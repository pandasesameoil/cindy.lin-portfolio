[Ding-Go](https://www.ding-go.com/) is a cross-border marketplace focused on bringing high-demand Taiwanese products and emerging Asian brands to consumers in the U.S. market.

The platform combines e-commerce, creator-driven marketing, and community-based discovery to help international brands localize and grow overseas through digital commerce and partnership ecosystems.

## My Role

At [Ding-Go](https://www.ding-go.com/), I worked across growth strategy, marketplace operations, creator partnerships, logistics optimization, and cross-border brand expansion initiatives.

## Platform Repositioning & Market Strategy

Through competitor analysis and market positioning strategies, I helped reposition the platform around high-demand Taiwanese imports and consumer trends within Asian-American and international communities.

## Revenue Growth

Within 7 months, we scaled monthly revenue from $2K to $30K by optimizing product selection, improving user experience, refining merchandising strategy, and executing growth-focused initiatives across marketing and operations.

## Logistics Optimization

I also helped rebuild logistics infrastructure by relocating fulfillment operations to the West Coast, reducing average shipping lead times from 20 days to 7 days and significantly improving customer experience and operational efficiency.

## Creator Partnerships & Growth Marketing

On the growth marketing side, I developed an influencer partnership framework that segmented creators based on brand awareness, engagement, and retention objectives. Through affiliate-based collaborations with 9 creators, campaigns generated over 200K combined organic Instagram views and contributed to 30% order growth.

## Cross-Border Expansion

In addition, I supported cross-border supplier and partnership expansion efforts by onboarding 5 US-based brands and coordinating vendor relationships across Taiwan, Japan, and the United States.

I also helped launch a new Beauty & Wellness category through international vendor partnerships, contributing to a 15% increase in platform revenue.

## Key Learnings

Through this experience, I gained hands-on exposure to marketplace growth, creator economy partnerships, cross-border commerce operations, localization strategy, supply chain optimization, and consumer-focused startup execution within fast-scaling environments.

## Overview

At Sunstone Management VC, I worked across investor relations, partnerships, event operations, and community growth to support startup ecosystem initiatives across Southern California.

## Event Operations & Community Growth

I helped organize and scale 100+ startup and investor events, including weekly startup demo days, NVIDIA GTC side events, CES founder events, private investor networking gatherings, Irvine Dream Run, and Irvine Tech Day community activations. These events brought together founders, investors, operators, students, and local business communities.

One of the largest projects I supported was **Irvine Dream Run**, where participation grew from 300 to 1,200 attendees across 3 annual events through sponsorship outreach, partnership coordination, and community marketing efforts.

I also helped manage **38+ partnerships** for Irvine Tech Day across universities, startup organizations, chambers of commerce, and local government groups.

## Investor Relations & Operations

In addition to community initiatives, I supported investor relationship operations across **16 active funds** and **100+ portfolio companies** by managing reporting workflows, investor communications, and Airtable-based tracking systems for relationship management and investment timelines.

## Cross-Border Initiatives

I also contributed to cross-border startup initiatives, including supporting a **$900k investment opportunity** for an artificial heart startup by helping connect a Chinese medical device industrialist with a US-based R&D team for manufacturing and commercialization discussions.

## Key Learnings

Through this experience, I developed hands-on experience in partnerships, event coordination, investor relations, startup operations, marketing, and community-led growth within fast-moving venture and startup environments.
